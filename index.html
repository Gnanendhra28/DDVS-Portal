Why Choose DDVS
Everything You Need,
In One Place

A complete digital platform replacing manual paperwork with secure, transparent, and fast processing.

🔐

Secure Verification

All submitted documents undergo multi-layer digital verification with tamper-proof audit trails and unique application IDs.

⚡

Fast Processing

Applications are processed digitally within 24 hours instead of weeks. No more physical visits for routine certificates.

📲

Real-Time Tracking

Citizens receive SMS and portal notifications at every step — submitted, under review, approved or rejected with reasons.

📊

Admin Dashboard

Officers get a centralized view of all pending applications, enabling efficient queue management and accountability.

🏛️

Multi-Department

Supports certificates across Revenue, MRO, Village Secretariat, Ward offices — all under one unified portal.

🌿

Paperless & Green

Eliminate physical forms, reduce storage costs, and contribute to a sustainable e-governance ecosystem.

How It Works
Simple 4-Step Process
1

Register & Login

Create your citizen account with Aadhaar-linked mobile number for secure access.

2

Submit Documents

Fill the application form, upload required documents and photos digitally.

3

Officer Review

Designated officer verifies documents and either approves or requests corrections.

4

Download Certificate

Receive digitally signed certificate via portal and SMS upon approval.
